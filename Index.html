We are committed to value growth, advancing innovation, and increasing capability in agriculture and food production

Our values

Passion

We are passionate about agriculture and food production. We bring a unique mix of global experience and success in operational and executive experience across the whole supply chain.

Performance

Our team combines over 100 years of combined experience in the field. We have proven capability spanning five continents in food production and farming operations, executive and change management, policy development, innovation and design.

Purpose

Delivering sustainable profitable growth to clients and investors is our goal. We believe that by making an impact in rural communities, and growing opportunities with purpose we will achieve ambitious growth targets.

Possibility

From our office in Ukraine we assist clients and investors achieve their objectives and efficiently gains across the Black Sea Region and CIS countries.

Focus

Advisory

Black Earth Innovation works with clients to help transform their operations to help guarantee agility, market relevance and profitable growth. We specialise on formulating turn-key agri-food supply chain solutions oriented towards export markets and consumer preferences.
For over 25 years Black Earth Innovation directors have advised investment managers, International Financial Institutions (IFIs), agri-food businesses, Non-Governmental Organisations and governments, in due-diligence, business re-restructuring, business planning, operations management, and technical assistance.
Our team has implemented projects to help advance know-how, best practice, and the creation of new business models in agriculture and food production.
We have produced food products or delivered projects for some of the world’s largest food companies, and Europe’s leading supermarket retail chains.

Asset Management

Our management team have a passion for agriculture and food production. We couple our knowledge gained through decades of practical experience globally, with proven track record of success within the agri-food sector. We pride ourselves our recognised capability to deliver operational excellence and client satisfaction within the industry.
We work with clients to increase their operational performance within the field and in the factory across both brown-field and green-field operations. We leverage our technical and planning expertise to realise projects in agriculture and food production from concept to realisation.
A key focus of our project methodology is to ensure scalable, profitable, and sustainable growth. Consequently, we place a disciplined focus on sales and marketing development, cost-price supply chain, and consumer awareness on all of our projects.

Investment

Black Earth Innovations operational team is focused on growing its vegetable farming, washing and packing operations in Ukraine. We are currently raising funds to expand these operations, and to further add value in further food processing and marketing of production in the Black Sea region. We are also piloting switch-grass renewable energy projects on behalf of eastern investors.
We work with investment partners in Ukraine to leverage equity investments by using our international expertise and expertise to drive performance through enhanced operational management, developing human capability, and improving cost-price efficiency in the supply chain.
Our expert team possess unique capabilities and knowledge in farm equipment and machinery. Our expert team help farmers and agri-holdings add value to their operational investments by sourcing suitable quality used farm equipment and machinery from trusted partners across Europe.

Bohdan Chomiak

Partner & Chairman

Mr. Bohdan Chomiak has 25 years of business and operational experience in Ukraine, CEE and CIS counties. Over the last five years he has facilitated commercial transactions in Ukraine’s agricultural sector with a value exceeding US$500m, providing advice to Asian, European and US companies, sovereign wealth funds, IFI’s, and private equity firms. Prior to coming to Ukraine, he was Dean of Extension at Olds College, Canada’s premier agricultural college, and had a 2,000 hectare grain and beef farm. While in Ukraine he managed a 20,000 hectare grain and oilseed farm. He led over $150m of technical assistance programs for USAID & Soros Foundation across former Soviet Union Countries. He speaks English, Ukrainian, and Russian.

Tom O’Callaghan

Partner & CEO

Tom O’Callaghan has 20 years of international management and process optimization experience spanning five continents. He is the former CEO of ICOS, the largest agri-food co-op lobbying organization in the British Isles, representing members with combined sales of $15bn per annum. Mr. O’Callaghan has helped optimize operations or produced products for, many of the world’s Top-20 Food Companies, and, eight of the worlds Top-10 dairy companies. He has lead new innovations within the agri-food industry globally in the areas of manufacturing process optimization, vocational training, corporate governance, agri-tech, and digital media. He holds BSc in Food Technology from the University of Limerick, and certificates in Corporate Governance from Dublin City Ryan Academy, and in Purchasing & Materials Management from Athlone Institute of Technology.

Thomas Carpenter

Partner & COO

Thomas Carpenter is a fourth generation farmer. He is also Managing Director of Ezy Technology which is an R&D company specialising in the design and manufacture of innovative Agri Machinery. He has farming interests in Ireland, UK, and Ukraine. He is has over 25 years in the Farming / Agricultural Machinery business. Thomas has designed, supplied & installed production and packing equipment for most all of the supermarket packers in Ireland. Mr Carpenter has supplied fresh produce to some of Europe’s largest supermarket retail chains. In Ukraine he is growing vegetables to EU retail Quality control and packaging standards. He has an Engineering Degree from Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT).

Sergey Bebko

Director

Sergey Bebko has a successful track record as a trader, analyst, project manager, and executive in grain & oilseeds trading, and, crude vegetable oils transshipment. Responsibilities included securing credits from international banks and other financial institutions, including IFC. He has overseen company strategy formulation, and the development of €100 million investments in new deep-water vegetable oils transshipment terminals & multi-seeds crushing plant capacity. Prior to his career in commercial trading Mr Bebko deployed multi-million dollar projects for international development institutions including USAID, US-Ukraine Foundation, and, Chemonics Inc. Sergey holds qualifications from Kiev Technological Institute of Food Industry, the Institute of Investment Management, Ukraine, and, Esbjerg Business University in Denmark. He speaks English, Spanish, Ukrainian, and Russian.

Vasyl Achkevych

Director

Sviatoslav Yurash

Director

Sviatoslav Yurash is an active participant in Ukrainian civil society, communications, and Public Relations. He is Co-Founder of the Home of the Free People, Ukrainian representative of the Ukrainian World Congress, Secretary of the Civil Council for the Parliamentary Committee on Foreign Affairs, and member of the Expert Board of the Ministry of Defence of Ukraine. As a PR expert Sviatoslav served as communications coordinator at Western NiS Enterprise Fund, producer for FOX News, and, Founder & Head of Euromaidan PR. His academic education encompasses studies at the University of Warsaw, Poland and University of Calcutta, India. He speaks English, Ukrainian, and Russian.
